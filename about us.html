About Us

Our journey from a humble beginning to a fully automated future — Sai Prasad Paper Products is now recognized as one of the leading manufacturers of disposable paper products. We produce high-quality, eco-friendly, and food-grade paper items made from recycled food-grade raw materials.

Due to the consistent quality of our products, we receive a large number of inquiries. Customers always choose our products because they trust the safety and quality we provide. We serve wholesalers, suppliers, distributors, retailers, and direct customers with equal dedication. Every time, we ensure the delivery of excellent products that not only meet high standards but also prioritize the health and safety of our customers.

Our Journey

2010 - The Beginning

We began operations with a single foot press machine and no investment for raw materials. With dedication and hard work, our journey started.

2014 - Rapid Growth

We added more semi-automatic machines and expanded our product range while maintaining the best quality in the market.

2016 - Expansion

We shifted to automation and built our first distributor network, supplying cities like Baramati, Indapur, Akluj, Saswad, Jejuri, and more.

2017 - Supplier Network

We fully installed automated machinery and began selling raw materials. A supplier from Karnataka expanded our reach across Solapur, Barshi, Latur, Kurduwadi, and surrounding areas.

2019 - Statewide Reach

Our products gained traction outside Maharashtra. We saw a rise in inquiries due to our consistent quality and eco-friendly approach.

2025 - Present

We continue our commitment to every distributor, supplier, wholesaler, retailer, and direct customer—delivering quality, trust, and sustainability.

Our Vision

Our Mission

At Sai Prasad Paper Products, our mission is to drive a sustainable future through eco-conscious innovation.
